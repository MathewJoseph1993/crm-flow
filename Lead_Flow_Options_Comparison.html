Why Option A?

  • Solves the problem Kapil mentioned 4-5 times: "HubSpot is broken, too much junk"
  • Sales team focuses on hot leads: Higher close rates, better morale
  • Scalable architecture: As volume grows 10x, CRM stays clean
  • Flexible design: Can always toggle to Option B if needed (it's just a filter)
  • Industry best practice: Top sales orgs use "reply-based CRM entry"

The Key Difference

Option A: CRM entry = Reply-gated (only engaged leads)
Option B: CRM entry = Qualification-gated (all qualified, regardless of reply)

Trade-off: Focus (Option A) vs Visibility (Option B)

How we mitigate risk:

1. Weekly audits of "Unqualified" leads to catch false negatives
2. Transparent scoring visible in Clay (no black box)
3. Manual review queue for borderline leads (60-79 score)
4. Monthly calibration with sales team feedback
5. Can switch to Option B anytime if needed (1-day toggle)

Bottom Line

Option A builds the foundation for scaling from $20M to $50M ARR.
Option B repeats the problems we're trying to solve.
